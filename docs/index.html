About Me

Born in 1995 in Lawrenceville, Georgia and raised in Suwanee, Georgia. I got my first job as a lifeguard when I turned 15, and after saving up for an entire summer, I built my first PC. This is where my passion for computers began. I knew I wanted to work with hardware and/or software from that moment forward, and I persued that from the moment I graduated high school. Through my education, I know I want to go into the field of embeddded engineering. I was introduced to assembly in my Embedded Systems Design class, and I immediately took to it. I love the small differences between regular software programming and programming microcontrollers. The world of embedded systems is vast and takes tangible real-world data from instruments -- something I find to be extremely engaging.
