About Me

Georgia native -- born and raised in metro Atlanta. In 2013, I attended my first semester of college at the University of North Georgia in Dahlonega, GA. After three semesters in the Computer Science program at UNG, I transferred to the University of Georgia to persue a degree in Computer Engineering. Through my time in school, I discovered that I most liked embedded design. I was introduced to assembly in my Embedded Systems Design class, and I immediately took to it. I enjoyed my time coding firmware in assembly for the Motorola 68HC11, and would love to persue a career as a firmware engineer.
